About Me

I'm a researcher focused on collaborative Virtual Reality (VR), physiological synchrony, and user experience. My work integrates eye-tracking, heart rate, and sentiment analysis to understand how people collaborate and engage in immersive XR environments.

Research & Projects

  • 💬 Conversational Speech Synchrony – Studying human–AI emotional alignment in immersive environments.
