About Me

I'm a researcher and educator working at the intersection of collaborative Virtual Reality (VR), physiological synchrony, and user experience. My research integrates eye-tracking, heart rate, and sentiment analysis to understand how people collaborate and engage in immersive XR environments. Alongside research, I teach Unity C# programming and Computer Illustrated Graphics to equip learners with practical and creative technical skills.

Research & Projects

🧠 Physiological Synchrony in Collaborative VR
