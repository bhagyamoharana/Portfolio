About Me

I'm a researcher focused on collaborative Virtual Reality (VR), physiological synchrony, and user experience. My work integrates eye-tracking, heart rate, and sentiment analysis to understand how people collaborate and engage in immersive XR environments.

Research & Projects

🧠 Physiological Synchrony in Collaborative VR
Investigating heart rate & pupil dilation synchrony to measure team dynamics and mental workload.
🎭 Audience–AI Synchrony
Analysing engagement in immersive theatre using physiological and motion data.
