About Me

I’m a researcher exploring collaborative VR and physiological synchrony to understand how people work together in immersive environments. My work combines eye-tracking, heart rate analysis, and sentiment modelling to evaluate cognitive load, engagement, and user experience in XR.

Research & Projects

  • 🧠 Physiological Synchrony in Collaborative VR – Investigating HR & pupil dilation synchrony to measure team dynamics.
  • 🎭 Audience–AI Synchrony – Analysing engagement in immersive theatre using physiological and motion data.
  • 🌊 Cea-SAR Flood Intelligence Platform – Forecasting floodplain dynamics with Synthetic Aperture Radar & GEE.
  • 💬 Conversational Speech Synchrony – Studying human–AI emotional alignment in immersive environments.
